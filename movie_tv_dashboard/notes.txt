1. Trending movies today
https://api.themoviedb.org/3/trending/movie/day?api_key=YOUR_API_KEY

2. Trending shows today
https://api.themoviedb.org/3/trending/tv/day?api_key=YOUR_API_KEY

3. Search by movie name
https://api.themoviedb.org/3/search/movie?api_key=YOUR_API_KEY&query=John%20Wick

4. Search by TV show name
https://api.themoviedb.org/3/search/tv?api_key=YOUR_API_KEY&query=Breaking%20Bad

5. Browse movies by genre

Step 1 — get list of movie genres:

https://api.themoviedb.org/3/genre/movie/list?api_key=YOUR_API_KEY


Step 2 — browse movies by genre (example: Action = 28)

https://api.themoviedb.org/3/discover/movie?api_key=YOUR_API_KEY&with_genres=28

6. Browse shows by genre

Step 1 — get list of TV show genres:

https://api.themoviedb.org/3/genre/tv/list?api_key=YOUR_API_KEY


Step 2 — browse shows by genre (example: Comedy = 35)

https://api.themoviedb.org/3/discover/tv?api_key=YOUR_API_KEY&with_genres=35

7. View top rated movies
https://api.themoviedb.org/3/movie/top_rated?api_key=YOUR_API_KEY
